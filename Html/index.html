WAKEY WAKEY!

WAKEY WAKEY! is an app designed to wake you up. The app allows you to choose your alarm, volume and sound and will keep going off every minute until you open your phone and solve the math problem. The app also contains a "Tunes" feature with custom made alarm tones. The app is entirely free to use.
