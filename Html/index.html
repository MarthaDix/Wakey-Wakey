WAKEY WAKEY!

WAKEY WAKEY! is an app designed to wake you up. The app allows you to choose your alarm, volume/sound that will keep going off every minute until you open your phone solve a math problem. The app also contains a "Tunes" feature with custom made alarm tones. The app is entirely free to use.
